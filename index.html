Bread Chase

Goal

Collect bread from the geese as a little robot. But beware, they are very aggressive. Collect all the bread in one area and move to the next by running through the game border. The more bread you collect, more geese will come after you.

The game ends once you lose all your health, indicated at the bottom left corner of the screen.

Reach 30 points and move on to the Snow Level, which has snow and an alternative background music.

Basic Controls

  • Movement: use the "wasd" or "arrow keys" to move.

  • Music: click the music button or press "m" to toggle music.

  • Reload Page: click the reload button or press "r" to reload the page.

  • Hide Instructions: click the hide instructions button to hide this.

Cheats

  • +points: "=" to increase points.

  • -points: "-" to decrease points.

  • Godmode: "0" to toggle invincibility.

  • Stop geese: "9" to toggle stopping geese.

  • Restore Health: "8" to regain all health.

  • Toggle Knockback: "7" to toggle knockback.

  • Freeze: "6" to freeze game.
